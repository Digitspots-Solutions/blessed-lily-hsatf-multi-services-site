About Us

Duds House Drilling Services Co (also operating as Duds House Wellhead Solutions Limited) is a premier oil and gas service provider based in Owerri, Imo State. We specialize in comprehensive drilling operations and wellhead solutions for the Nigerian petroleum industry.

Led by experienced HSE management professionals, our company is built on a foundation of operational excellence, safety integrity, and technical expertise. We understand the demanding nature of oil and gas operations and are committed to delivering services that meet international standards while maintaining the highest safety protocols.

Our strategic location in Owerri positions us to serve operators across the Niger Delta and surrounding regions with rapid response times and local expertise. We combine technical proficiency with a deep understanding of Nigeria's regulatory environment and operational challenges.

At Duds House, safety is not just a priority—it's our culture. Every project is executed with meticulous attention to health, safety, and environmental standards, ensuring protection for our personnel, clients, and communities.

Our Services

Drilling Operations

Comprehensive drilling services including land rig operations, directional drilling support, and drilling fluids management. Our experienced crews ensure efficient operations while maintaining strict safety and environmental compliance.

Wellhead Solutions

Complete wellhead installation, maintenance, and integrity management services. We provide expert solutions for wellhead equipment supply, installation supervision, and ongoing maintenance programs.

HSE Management

Professional Health, Safety, and Environment management services including HSE planning, risk assessment, safety audits, and regulatory compliance. Our HSE programs meet Nigerian DPR requirements and international oil and gas standards.

Drilling Supervision

Experienced drilling supervision services providing on-site oversight, quality control, and technical support to ensure operations meet specifications and industry best practices.

Equipment Supply

Reliable supply of quality drilling and wellhead equipment, tools, and consumables. We maintain relationships with trusted manufacturers to ensure availability and technical support.

Technical Consultation

Expert consultation on drilling programs, wellhead design, safety systems, and operational optimization for oil and gas operators throughout Nigeria.
